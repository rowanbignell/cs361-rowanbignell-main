What is this?

Tomato Tracker uses the pomodoro technique to help you stay focused. The pomodoro technique consists of "pomodoros" which start with a focus session of 25 minutes and ends with a short break of 5 minutes. Every 4 pomodoros is followed by a long break of 25mins.


Click [START] to start the pomodoro. If the timer is running you can hit [STOP] to stop the timer. Whether you are in a long break, short break, or focus session is tracked below the timer. Clicking the [LONG BREAK], [FOCUS], and [SHORT BREAK] buttons will skip to the corrosponding timer. The colors of the page will change depending on which timer is active.
